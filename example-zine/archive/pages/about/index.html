About

In the interconnected tapestry of human experiences, moments pass like fleeting whispers, leaving behind only fragments of memories and emotions. As an artist, I find solace and inspiration in the realm of visual storytelling, where images hold the power to capture and preserve these ephemeral glimpses of existence. Through my archive of collected images transformed into a webzine, titled "Traces of Time," I embark on a journey to explore the profound essence of our shared human narrative.


The concept of an archive embodies the notion of preservation and reverence for the past. It serves as a vessel that safeguards precious relics, allowing us to reflect upon our history, culture, and individual stories. In my quest to assemble a rich tapestry of images, I have embarked on a relentless pursuit of moments frozen in time, capturing a kaleidoscope of emotions, perspectives, and narratives.
